  1. What is Campus Travelr?

    2. Campus Travelr is a new business focused on getting rid of getting lost on campus or not being able to find a parking spot. We were founded in March 2016 with the purpose of fixing navigation and parking problems at UAlbany. We will soon be coming out with apps and technologies that will hopefully be implemented at the university soon.

  2. What makes you different from your competitors?

    3. The main difference between us and our competitors is that we are completely Free and Open Source. All of our source code will be available online on our website and on Github and Gitlab. We will also be significantly cheaper and be better about privacy and security of our users data and sensitive information.
